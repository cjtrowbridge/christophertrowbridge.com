Recent Magical History

The recent history of magic begins with the magical rennaissance of the nineteenth century and works towards today.

Topics covered include: Gerald Gardner and his relationship to Wicca; Aleister Crowley and his relationship to Thelema and Astrum Argentum.

Astrum Argentum leads to a discussion about Kabbala and Theravada.

Topics covered include

Thaumaturgy

The study of non-religious magic is perhaps the most important core principle within the field.

Healing Magic

We explore healing magic from the perspective of contemporary Gardnerian traditions.

We explore the connection to Theravada.

Bindings and Wards

We explore magical barriers and wards of protection including spells for locations, tattoos, amulets, etc.

Astral Projection and Lucid Dreaming

We discuss techniques and objectives. Listen to presentations from practitioners.

Magical Langauge and Symbology

We explore popular magical writing systems like Enochian, Elder Futhark, Theban, Sindarin, and Ogham.

We look at glossolalia and xenoglossia.

Lab: LBRP

In this laboratory session, we learn about and then execute the lesser banishing ritual of the pentagram.
